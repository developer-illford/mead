Our Company

MEAD Constructions is a full-service Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) firm specializing in RCC and Pre-Engineered Buildings. We deliver complete civil and structural solutions—from design and procurement to construction, interiors, and maintenance. Our integrated approach ensures faster delivery, seamless coordination, and top-quality results.

MEAD Interior Design LLC, Dubai, established in 2011, is a multidisciplinary interior design and space planning firm. Based in Al Qusais, we serve a wide range of clients—from private residences and luxury villas to retail, F&B outlets, corporate offices, and hospitality projects. With expertise in architecture, design, and project management, we blend creativity and functionality to deliver inspiring spaces.

What types of construction projects does MEAD Constructions specialize in?

MEAD Constructions specializes in Reinforced Cement Concrete (RCC) buildings and Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEBs), delivering innovative and durable structures.

Do you provide end-to-end services from design to maintenance?

Yes, we offer complete EPC solutions, covering design, procurement, construction, interior fit-out, and life-cycle maintenance under one roof.

How does MEAD ensure quality and timely project delivery?

We follow strict quality standards, integrated project management, and efficient coordination to deliver projects on time without compromising excellence.

Can MEAD handle both RCC buildings and Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEBs)?

Absolutely, our team has specialized expertise in both RCC and PEB construction, ensuring tailored solutions for each project type.
